Noah's Runch

This is the first recipe I made on my own! I love to cook and experiment. I made this to go with my brother's fried rice. It's the perfect combination of sour and rancid!

No chef's arsenal is complete without this all purpose ingredient

Ingredients

  • 1 gallon Milk
  • 1 cup whole black peppercorns
  • 1 can of MtnDew pitch black
  • crust

Steps

  1. In a large bowl or stand mixer combine the gallon of milk and can of MtnDew pitch black
  2. Whip this mixture until it is homogenous and fluffy
  3. Slowly fold in the whole black peppercorns to taste
  4. Sprinkle in some crust on the top and it's finished!

    5. I recommend bottling this and keep it refridgeerated. It should last a year in the fridge!
