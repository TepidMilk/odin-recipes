Chicken Soup

Grandpa's chicken soup

This chicken soup recipe is very near and dear to me. My grandfather would make this soup for me anytime I was a naughty boy!

I recommend making this recipe for any naughty boys who are in your life

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Water
  • Love
  • 3 20mg tablets of adderall

Steps

  1. Add chicken and water into a dutch oven on high and bring to a boil
  2. Once boiling turn heat to low and allow dish to simmer until there is no more water
  3. During this time grind up the 3 20mg tablets of adderall into a fine powder
  4. After all the water has evaporated, add in the adderall powder
  5. Enjoy! :)
