Brother's Fried Rice

This is my brother's famous fried rice recipe. He learned to make this when he was around 5 years old! Since then he has been constantly tweaking and modifying it to what it is today.

If you want to impress guests with something simple and quick try this one out!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup Runch
  • Mayo (optional)

Steps

  1. crack and beat two eggs into a bowl.
  2. Mix beaten eggs with rice.
  3. put mixture in a pot on medium heat.
  4. let the rice and eggs cook until brown.
  5. Once browned add in your Runch and mix.
  6. let this sit on low heat for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionaly.
  7. Your fried rice is now ready! Serve in a bowl and if you like it extra creamy like me add some Mayo!
